    About The Event

    It's an event of 3 days, exclusively dedicated to programmers, including talks, workshops, a competition which is the first physical competitive programming organized by students of CSE club, provided with qualified speakers and trainers. The event aims to help both beginners and advanced programmers.

    About Us

    CSE -Club Scientifique de l'ESI- is a student club at the
    Higher National School of Computer Science, Algiers.
    It is created for computer science, high technology,
    robotics, design and anything else technology-related
    enthusiasts.
    CSE's activities made it establish itself on a national &
    international scale and became one of the country's
    largest & most active clubs.
